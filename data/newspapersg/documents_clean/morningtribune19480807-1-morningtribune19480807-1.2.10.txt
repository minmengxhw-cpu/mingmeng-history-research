CHINA INVASION |
REPORT DENIED
Rangoon, Aug. 6.—The China
Democratic League, outlawed
by the Chinese Government, to-
day denied that it was organis-
ing an armed force on the
Burma border for a possible in-
-vasion of China.
It is described reports to this
effect as “an invention by the
reactionary Nanking Govern-
-ment.”—Reuter.

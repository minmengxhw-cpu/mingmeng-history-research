reforms brought about an almost complete liquidation of fesdalism. In other ; : i 
areas (North China), land reforms were introduced later, for the most part in : : 
1949; in still other areas (Central and South China), land reforms are just be- | 
ing introduced. In certain areas, China is still engaged in the policy of re= :

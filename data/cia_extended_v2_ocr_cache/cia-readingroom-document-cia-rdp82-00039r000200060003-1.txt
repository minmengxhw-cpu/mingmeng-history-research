: HIGH TIDE OF PATRIOTISM SURGES THROUGHOUT THR LAND 


American aggression in Taiwan and in the air over the Northeast and 
Shantung has aroused the peopla of China from the Yalu to Sinkiang and from 
Mongolia to Hainan, On 4 November, various democratic parties of our 
country issued a joint manifesto which stated that China's aid to Korea is 
being offered for self-defense and not for altruistic reason, 

During recent weeks, the press in Peiping has been playing up the 
heroism of the volunteers in Korea and the labor heroes on the home front, 
All sorts of organizations, moreover, have been holding pep meetings, These 
show the extent of patriotic tide now prevailing throughout the land, laborers 
in Peiping have been flooding newspapers with letters and articles declaring 
that, while they are all for peace, they will defend the country to their 
death against any aggression, One worker wrote in his own blood: "I shall 
go to the aid of Korea and will not return till the Americans are beaten into 
dust", : 

Students in all schools in all parts of the country have been declaring 
their loyalty to the country and have been organizing patriotic groups, At 
a meeting at the Peeple's University in Peiping, old-time revolutionaries, 
some with 30 years of experience, have renewed their vows of iovantes Farmers

In a meeting held on 27 October 1950, six representative bodies of the 
Northeast supported Chou En-lai's warning to the enemy given on 26 October 
1950 and reiterated their determination not to let aggressors approach or 
cross the boundary of the Northeast. Workers in Mukden and An-tung, dwellers

stimulus of patriotic emulation drive, the laborers of the Northeast have 
produced 314.4 parcent of last year's production within three quarters of 
1950, They hope to exceed the fourth quarter's production goal. 

Workers of 49 factories in all sorts of industries have answered the 
25 November /sic ~ presumably October/ challenge for higher production by 
model laborer Chao Kuo-yu and his team, His team, incidentally, is already

almost one-half. In the Mukden Steel Works, the firing team of batch of 
steel cut the time required from 5 hours 20 minutes to 4 hours 47 minutes 
on the day that higher production pledge was made, Four batches of steel

893.00/10–2847: Telegram

[Document 268]

The Ambassador in China (Stuart) to the Secretary of State

Nanking, October 28, 1947.
[Received October 28—8:12 a.m.]

2157. Reference Embtel 2149, October 28. Shanghai afternoon papers report Lo Lung-chi, Democratic League leader, hiding in American Embassy. These reports have no foundation in fact.

Stuart

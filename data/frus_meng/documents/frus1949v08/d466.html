893.00B/6–2249: Telegram

[Document 466]

The Ambassador in China (Stuart) to the Secretary of State

Nanking, June 22, 1949.
[Received June 22—6:02 a. m.]

1346. Local press June 20 reports Chang Lan, Shih Liang, Lo Lung-chi, “Wang Pao-chang, Kuo Chen-tao,38 others arrived in Nanking June 19 on way to Peiping. Chang, Lo, Shih, press states, aside from attending preparatory committee of new PCC will also attend fourth plenary session of Democratic League.

Sent Department, repeated Peiping 231, Shanghai 739, OffEmb Canton unnumbered.

Stuart

38 All Democratic League members; they reached Peiping on June 24.
